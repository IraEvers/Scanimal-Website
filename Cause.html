Our Cause

Scanimal is meant to educate childeren in a fun way about wildlife. We are aiming to make a trip to a zoo or park even more fun by adding more intercation to the trip! With the help of Scanimal we can reduce the amount of litter within the zoo's, as the app will already contain a map of the zoo digitally. It will also help to raise awareness about the endangered species.
